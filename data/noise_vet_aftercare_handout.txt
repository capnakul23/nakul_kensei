Birchwood Animal Hospital - After-visit care notes

- Offer water; keep meals normal unless we told you otherwise.
- A little tiredness the day of vaccines is normal; watch for swelling at
  the injection site and call us if it persists past 48 hours.
- Keep nails and ears clean between visits; brush teeth a few times a week.
- Fresh water and shade on warm walks; never leave a dog in a parked car.
